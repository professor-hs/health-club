About Health Club

“Talent wins games, but teamwork and hard work win championships”. We're the Health Club at VIT our mission remains straightforward by helping all students maintain their well-being along with their happiness. Orchestrating health and wellness workshops alongside event organization serves as our mission to foster greater health awareness through better behavioral choices. Whether it's physical fitness, mental health, or emotional well-being, we're here to create a supportive space where everyone can thrive. Follow us for tips, resources, and updates as we work together toward a healthier community!

Events'23

  • Health Awareness Campaign - August 2023
  • On the International Day of Girl Child, Health club brings you an awareness on the women health issues. Health needs of man and woman are slightly different even though the life styles are almost the same. Some health issues may be common to both the genders, yet woman is at a higher risk than man. This can be due to psychological differences, minor differences in the life style and many others. Let us hear some of these from our expert Dr. Nidhya N Joghee who is working on few of these issues, related to woman health.
